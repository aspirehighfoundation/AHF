Projects of Aspire High Foundation



Offline Learning

At AHF, we rely on the truth that the most effectiveness of teaching comes from the thoughts the teacher has had during the entire time of his or her existence in the classroom. We believe in ACTIVE LEARNING where students have contact with instructors which helps in maintaining interpersonal relationships ­

Traditional classroom learning helps children stay focused and motivated throughout their coaching period. Some students don’t have access to technological devices, so we use traditional classroom environment, where learning begins with the teacher sharing his knowledge with students, proceeds with discussions with them.

  • In AHF, we intake a limited number of students and give them proper coaching with the appropriate study material which covers the entire syllabus. The material we prepare helps children in cracking the Entrance exams with an ease. The instructor will be available in the class to clarify the doubts in the particular session.
  • We visit orphanages, underdeveloped schools and select students from class 5th to class 12th.
  • We provide notes and material for each subject. We also conduct practice tests regularly.


    • Practical Learning

    ”Tell me and I forget, teach me and I may remember, involve me and I learn.” - Benjamin Franklin

    Many students learn more easily by actually ‘doing’ activities. Through our offline teaching we educate students only with theoretical knowledge. Involving in a practical activity helps structure a lesson and improve engagement and knowledge of a student.

         Can you imagine someone teaching you to swim within four walls of classroom? It is Impossible! You have to get into water to learn swimming. In the same way subjects are skill based and practice oriented. Theoretical knowledge of subjects has to be supported by practical teaching approach in order to make students connect to a subject. With the help of this approach, we teach students, how to use theoretical knowledge practically in solving the real life problems.



    We implement Practical Learning in the following ways:

  • First, we start with the basic activities given in the NCERT textbooks and ICSE textbooks of each class from 6th to 12th.
  • We involve students in projects which solve day to day life problems.
  • Each group of students is monitored by volunteers who will help students to practice it for gaining deeper knowledge.


    • Why Practical Learning?

    We believe, “Theory without practice is valueless.”

  • Interactive education creates a deeper impact. Practical education is way more interactive than theoretical.
  • Learning through practical sources instead of conventional theoretical ones, the learning process does become comparatively easier. Students find it more interesting which makes them to get their hands on the subject thus making it easier.
  • Learning about facts and theories are not really of any use unless you are aware of their application in real life situation.  Unless you are applying your theoretical knowledge to real life situations through practical experiments, theory doesn’t hold much good and you will keep wondering that why you are studying that particular topic.
  • Practical learning is meant to improve your skills.


    • At AHF our students’ learning is our main objective. Thus we strive to give them a joyful learning, where they enjoy and grasp the subject effortlessly.

    Online coaching classes

    Along with offline teaching, we are also implementing online coaching classes. With this approach, we believe that we can educate students who are in any location and still be connected to them.

  • We have our SME’s (Subject Matter Experts) available to take online sessions regularly and provide the students with appropriate study materials.
  • We take the whole responsibility to train the students by providing them with all the requirements and stay connected with us.
  • Our subject matter experts will teach the students using an online platform to conduct online sessions.



    • Our online approach will be the same as our offline teaching process , instead we will connect to our students who are in a different location and ensure they still get a great exposure to our teaching. With the help of this approach, we are able to run our classes during this pandemic as well.
