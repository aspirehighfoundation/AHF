About Us



Welcome to Aspire High Foundation

At Aspire High Foundation (AHF) we believe that “Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world”. The main motive behind establishing Aspire High Foundation (AHF) is educating all students to achieve today and tomorrow in a global community.

     In some parts of our country, students are going to school every day. It’s their normal life. But in other parts of the country, we are starving for education...it’s like a precious gift. We believe in sharing our knowledge to the children who can’t afford high end schools. A team of people from various fields came together, joined their hands to establish Aspire High Foundation (AHF). We as a community, build relations with children and help them in shaping their future in the right track. Together at AHF we create a loving community of purposeful learning that focuses on the whole child, mind, and spirit.

Our AIM

At AHF, our main aim is to teach under-privileged students and give them a chance to level up their career opportunities. We also aim to bring people together who would like to contribute towards our society by shaping the young minds of tomorrow and give them a platform to fulfil it. We always focus on providing quality education to the students and ensure that they have great exposure to learn everyday. We believe that being goal oriented can make you work more towards what you want to achieve. That’s why we train our students in order to crack competitive exams, so that the students will be prepared for entrance exams like JEE Mains, JEE Advanced and NEET to get into their dream universities in future.

Our aim is not just to teach our students to read.

  • We teach them to question what they read, what they study.
  • We teach them to doubt.
  • We teach them to think.
  • We teach them to learn from their mistakes.
  • We teach them how to understand.
  • We teach them how to teach others.

    • How Does AHF work?

    Every parents dream is to see their child succeed in future. Every parent dreams their children to get into a top ranked colleges like IIT's, NIT's, Medical colleges, Agricultural Universities. In order to get into first tier universities there are a lot of entrance exams that have to be cracked. Can all the parents succeed in providing a proper coaching and preparation for their children without spending their whole wallet on coaching institutes and schools? It’s a NO. That’s where Aspire High Foundation (AHF) comes to play its role. This mainly deals with providing perfect career map to a child.

        Being an NGO we offer everything at a free of cost. Under the supervision of top educators we train students to crack competitive exams. We provide all the facilities required for a student to crack these exams. The concept we provide are crafted in a way that the children can crack any question very easily.
